Privacy Policy - CHDA Exam Prep

Last Updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

CHDA Exam Prep does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Quiz scores and performance history
  • Study progress and session data
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored using your device's secure local storage and never leaves your device.

No Account Required

The app does not require account creation, login, or any personal information.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

As no personal information is collected, this app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in app updates with user notification.
